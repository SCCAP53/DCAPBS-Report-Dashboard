DCAPBS Reports and Dashboards

Trouble acccessing a Qlikview or R-Connect Dashboard? Click here.



DCAPBS Provider RVU Productivity

Qlikview Outpatient Inpatient Schedule Productivity Compliance Billing

Dashboard displaying DCAPBS Provider RVUs and productivity metrics. Allows for filtering by provider (managers only), codes, departments, dates, etc.

Overdue Encounters Report

R-Connect Office Visits Compliance Outpatient Fellows

Reports on providers who have Office Visits that are overdue for closure based on DCAPBS Close Encounters Policy. Includes types of encounters and details about each visit and encounters routed to attendings for closure.

Unsigned Inpatient Notes

R-Connect Inpatient Compliance

Reports on providers who have inpatient notes that are overdue for being signed. Includes types of notes and details about each visit.

DCAPBS Office Visits Explorer

Qlikview Office Visits Schedule Outpatient

This dasbhoard allows users to explore data related to outpatient encounters (Office Visits). Several filters are available and data include demographic, department, provider, appointment, diagnosis, and billing details.

Ongoing Professional Practice Evaluation (OPPE)

Qlikview Outpatient Inpatient Schedule Productivity Compliance

Dashboard displaying Ongoing Professional Practice Evaluation (OPPE) data for DCAPBS. Includes metrics such as AVS, medical review, CSSRS, Chart Closing, and Note Closing. Provider and Department level data can be filtered.

3440 Outpatient Producivity

R-Connect Outpatient Productivity Compliance Billing

For 3440 providers only. Displays data regarding performance on RVU, new patient visits, and visit volumes.

Provider Schedule Availability

Qlikview Outpatient Schedule Productivity Office Visits

Dashboard outpatient past and scheduled appointments for the purpose of displaying provider and department scheduling availability. Displays slot utilization and reasons for slot closings.

Inpatient CSSRS Data

R-Connect Inpatient Clinical Tools

Report displaying CSSRS complaince for DCAPBS Inpatient Visits.

Healthy Minds Healthy Kids Program

Qlikview Specialty Office Visits Outpatient

This dasbhoard displays information relevant to providers and administrators in the HMHK Program. Includes visit and diagostis statistics, referral tracking, and insurance payor data.

DCAPBS Epic Login Departments

R-Connect Epic Settings

Lists data about DCAPBS Epic Departments. Includes contact, location, tax information. Data also grouped by setting and program.

Fellows Patient Transfer List

R-Connect Fellows

A complete list of patients from fellows' Epic Patient List. Used by coordinators to determine which patients to transfer patients to after training rotation ends.
